About Me

I am a dedicated Public Health undergraduate and aspiring global health researcher with a strong commitment to advancing evidence-based healthcare through research, scientific collaboration, teaching, and community engagement. My academic and professional journey has been shaped by hands-on experience in public health research, scientific publishing, academic leadership, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Currently, I serve as a Research Assistant at ABEx Bio-Research Center, where I contribute to research design, literature reviews, field data collection, data analysis, project coordination, and scientific writing. I also serve as a Student Associate at the Journal of Emerging Global Health, Daffodil International University, supporting academic publishing and journal-related activities.

Previously, I served as a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Development Studies, Daffodil International University, where I supported undergraduate students through academic mentoring, course coordination, assignment evaluation, and communication between faculty and students. This experience strengthened my teaching, leadership, and communication skills.

In addition, I work as an Editorial Assistant for an international scientific journal, contributing to manuscript management, peer-review coordination, editorial processes, and scientific publishing. As a co-author of multiple peer-reviewed international publications, my research interests include Public Health, Epidemiology, Environmental Health, Non-Communicable Diseases, Global Health, Health Systems, Health Policy, Climate Change, Migration, and Implementation Research.

My technical expertise includes SPSS-based data analysis, scientific writing, research documentation, research profile management, and scientific communication. I have also held leadership positions in student organizations and participated in public health volunteer initiatives, strengthening my teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving abilities.

My long-term goal is to contribute to international public health research, higher education, and evidence-informed health policy by collaborating with leading universities, research institutions, NGOs, and global organizations. I am actively seeking opportunities for research collaboration and fully funded Master's and PhD programs where I can make meaningful contributions through research, education, and innovation.
